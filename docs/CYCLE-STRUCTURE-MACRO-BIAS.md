# Cycle Structure & Macro Bias — Framework for Positioning

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-07

This document captures the framework for forming a macro bias and planning entries. It is not a prediction — it is a rough idea of direction on a timeframe for positioning purposes.

---

## 1. Bias Forming — Not Predicting

**Form bias → what I expect → what I bet on.**

Forming a bias is important as a framework for positioning. You're not making a prediction — you're forming a rough idea of the direction of the market on a certain timeframe.

If you have enough reasons to think the probability of a certain direction is good enough, you can decide to bet on that bias. You can start thinking about and planning your entry and invalidation.

A scenario ≠ a prediction.

---

## 2. Textbook Cycle Structure

Think in terms of **structure**, not time (e.g. 4-year cycle) or raw price change.

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Stage 1** | Bull |
| **Stage 2** | Bear (range low forms) |
| **Stage 3** | Accumulation below macro range (deviation) |
| **Stage 4** | Reclaim macro range & breakout of stage 3 range |
| **Stage 5** | Bull into range high, and eventually more parabolically into new highs (above macro range) |

Structure tells you:
- What to expect in terms of price action
- Where to enter in terms of risk/reward
- Where invalidation levels are
- Where to get out

It does **not** tell you how price will get there. Sometimes stage 4→5 plays out quickly and aggressively. Sometimes price retraces the entire macro range low reclaim pump back into the range low and grinds there for a year before completing the cycle.

**Mini cycles within macro cycles:** Within the bigger macro cycle, full mini cycles can form (e.g. stages 1–5 inside stages 4 and 5 of the macro cycle). Mini cycles can be savage (70%+ declines, 1+ year). Breakouts of mini cycle stage 3 often happen around key levels of the macro range.

## 5. Gold — Lead-Lag Relationship

Bitcoin and Gold are correlated, but not "going up together in lockstep."

- Often **inverted on immediate moves**
- On the bigger picture: **lead-lag** — money flows into Gold first, and at some point spills over into Bitcoin

**Historical pattern:**
- Bitcoin often goes **parabolic after Gold finishes** its parabolic move
- 2021: Gold front-ran Bitcoin. When Gold topped and entered stage 5 parabolic, Bitcoin was still forming a stage 3 bottom. Bitcoin corrected ~70% during Gold's parabolic phase. After Gold topped, Bitcoin ran from ~4k to ~68k.
- 2013: Bitcoin's first major cycle started after Gold topped and entered a multi-year correction

**Current:** Gold is in one of the biggest parabolic moves of our lifetime. Bitcoin has historically not gone parabolic while Gold is having one. Expectation: Bitcoin's parabolic phase more likely after Gold finishes.

**BTC/Gold chart:** Similar setup to previous cycle — below range low. Watch for reclaim and trigger. Not a fractal; it's macro cycle → mini cycle retrace → reversal at key level (range low) → continuation of macro cycle structure.

## 7. How to Enter — Three Scenarios

The macro bias tells you how to position, not how to time short or medium time frames.

**Current state:** Still in downtrend. Broke below macro range high (now resistance). No clear mini cycle stage 3 yet, no breakout.

**Plan:** Look for mini cycle stage 3 base + breakout trigger around a macro range key level.

### Scenario 1: Range High

- Range here for a while, take out lows — doesn't matter for positioning
- **Entry triggers:** Nice base forming (potential mini cycle stage 3) → breakout above base and above macro range high → reclaim of local range low → confluence with trendline
- **Invalidation:** Fall back below

### Scenario 2: Mid-Range

- Fall deeper right away, or fake out into resistance above, or reclaim then fail
- Stage 2 (bear) of mini cycle likely not finished yet
- Fall into mid-range key level before forming a base there

### Scenario 3: Range Low

- No reclaim anywhere, or reclaim fails shortly after
- Fall all the way back to range low zone, or briefly spike below
- More time ahead

**Principle:** Don't know which scenario we'll get. Don't know exactly how the path will form. Watch, adapt, update plans as new information comes in.
